Welcome to the Texarkana Muslim Community

The Texarkana Muslim Community needs your help building a mosque (masjid). In Texarkana, we have a strong and growing Muslim Community but we currently do not have a mosque to pray. We are currently praying temporarily in the living room of a house that belongs to one of our sisters. We need your help in the name of Allah (S.W.T.), we need your donations to help us build a mosque and a Community center for the Texarkana Muslim Community.

Donating for Masjid

You can donate as follows:

  1. Donate cash in boxes available in the masjid.
  2. Zelle transfer to the account at- Texarkanamuslimcommunity@gmail.com
  3. Through gofund- https://gofund.me/a5d92a97
